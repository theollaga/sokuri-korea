## Privacy Policy for Sokuri Korea

*Last updated: May 16, 2026*

This privacy policy explains how Sokuri Korea collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website (https://korea.sk-sokuri.com). By using our site, you agree to the practices described herein.

### Information We May Collect

We collect two main types of data:

1. **Voluntarily Provided Information**
   If you choose to send us an email, we will retain the email address and content of your message solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiry. We do not store this information in a database or use it for any other purpose.

2. **Automatically Collected Information**
   As you browse our site, certain technical data is gathered automatically through standard web server logs and analytics tools. This includes:
   - IP address (anonymized or truncated after a short period)
   - Browser type and device characteristics
   - Pages visited, referral source, and timestamps
   - Cookie identifiers

   This information helps us understand how our site is used, detect technical issues, and improve overall performance. It is not linked to your personal identity.

### Cookies

Sokuri Korea uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that remember your preferences and help us analyze site traffic.

We use the following types of cookies:

- **Essential cookies**: Necessary for basic site functionality (e.g., page loading, navigation).
- **Analytics cookies**: Collect aggregated, anonymous data about how visitors interact with our site (e.g., which pages are most popular).
- **Functionality cookies**: Remember your choices (such as language preference) to provide a more personalized experience.

We do not use cookies to store sensitive personal data, nor do we sell cookie-derived information to third parties.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the site's usability.

### Analytics Tools Usage

We may use analytics services such as Google Analytics or Cloudflare Web Analytics to collect aggregated, anonymized data about site usage. These services help us understand visitor demographics, behavior flow, and content performance. The data collected does not include personally identifiable information and is used solely for improving the site.

If analytics tools are employed, they operate under their own privacy policies, which you can review directly with the service provider.

### Google AdSense

Sokuri Korea displays advertisements through the Google AdSense network to support the operation of the site. When ads are served:

- Google may use cookies (including the DoubleClick DART cookie) to serve ads based on your visits to this site and other websites (interest-based advertising).
- No personal data from Sokuri Korea is shared with Google for ad targeting beyond what is typical for AdSense usage.
- Ads are clearly distinguishable from editorial content and do not influence our editorial decisions.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads) or by managing third-party cookies through your browser. For more information, see [Google's advertising policies](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads).

### AI-Generated Content
