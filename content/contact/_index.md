## Contact Information

You can reach the Sokuri Korea team at:

**Email:** contact@sk-sokuri.com

We read all messages and aim to respond within a few business days. Please note that we do not currently operate a contact form or social media accounts for this site.

## What to Include

To help us respond more quickly:

- **For corrections** — include the article title and the specific section or sentence
- **For topic suggestions** — briefly describe what you'd like us to cover
- **For business inquiries** — mention the nature of your inquiry in the subject line (e.g., partnership, advertising, content licensing)
- **For general feedback** — just share your thoughts; we read every message

## What We Cannot Help With

Sokuri Korea is a publication, not a personal service. We are unable to provide:

- Personal travel itineraries or trip planning
- Immigration, visa, or legal advice (please consult a licensed professional)
- Medical, financial, or other regulated advice
- Translation services
- Job placement or housing search

For these, we recommend consulting a licensed professional or specialized service.

For urgent matters or time-sensitive questions, please check our published guides first, as many common topics are already addressed in detail.

Thank you for your interest in Sokuri Korea — we look forward to hearing from you.
