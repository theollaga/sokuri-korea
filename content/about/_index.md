## Our Purpose

Sokuri Korea exists to bridge the gap between Korea and the rest of the world. We recognize that while interest in Korean culture is growing worldwide, reliable, easy-to-understand information in English can still be scattered or overly academic. Our goal is to provide clear, accurate, and practical guides that help foreigners truly engage with Korea—whether they are preparing for a visit, starting a new life here, or simply satisfying their curiosity from afar.

## Who We Serve

Our primary audience includes:
- Expats and newcomers settling in Korea
- Short-term visitors and tourists
- Students and researchers of Korean studies
- Professionals working with Korean partners
- Anyone worldwide with a genuine interest in Korean life

We write with the assumption that readers may be unfamiliar with Korean customs, language, or geography, so we explain concepts thoroughly without assuming prior knowledge.

## What We Cover

Sokuri Korea organizes content into five core sections, each designed to give you a well-rounded understanding of the country:

**Korean Culture**  
History, traditional arts, social norms, holidays, and everyday practices that reflect Korea’s rich heritage.

**Korea Travel**  
Destination guides, transportation systems, accommodation tips, seasonal events, and safety advice for traveling across the peninsula.

**Korean Food**  
From fermented staples like kimchi to regional delicacies, dining customs, food markets, and cooking basics.

**Korean Language**  
Introduction to Hangul, pronunciation guides, grammar essentials, and resources for learners at all levels.

**Modern Korea**  
Insights into Korea’s technological innovations, entertainment industry, fashion trends, urban development, and role on the global stage.

## Our Approach

All content is researched carefully and written with an international reader in mind. We strive to:
- Use clear, conversational English
- Provide context for cultural references
- Highlight practical tips you can apply immediately
- Cite reliable sources where appropriate
- Avoid stereotypes and present a balanced view

While we aim for accuracy, we encourage readers to verify critical information (such as visa requirements or transportation schedules) with official sources, as details can change over time.

## Stay Connected

If you have feedback, topic suggestions, or notice something that needs updating, please reach out. We value your input and use it to improve our guides for everyone.
